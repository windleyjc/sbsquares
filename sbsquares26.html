2026 Super Bowl LX Squares


Here is the place to purchase your Super Bowl squares and follow the payout action on game day.

Send me $10 per square via Venmo: @Jay-Windley
In the note field, let me know what initials or name you want in your square(s).
You can also let me know if you have a preference where on the grid you would like to be located.
However the numbers will be decided upon at random after the grid is filled.
Once the grid is filled, I will post the grid here.
I will keep payouts updated on this page.


Here is the payout plan.
Score at end of 1st Quarter: $100
Score at Halftime: $200
Socre at end of 3rd Quarter: $100
Every score change: $20 (see here for an example)
Remaining $ goes to Final Score. If it is a very low scoring game, the person having the final score could get between $400 and $500. If it breaks the record for highest scoring game ever, the person having the final score might get under $200. The final score is the score at the end of the game, not necessarily at the end of the 4th quarter (for example if there is overtime).

Check out this example of payouts to get a little clearer example of how things will work.
